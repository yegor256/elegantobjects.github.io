If you truly love OOP and agree with the premises of Elegant Objects, you must be in our gang. Want to join but don't know any member yet? Text @yegor256 in Telegram. If you don't follow the rules listed here, you will be ejected.

1. Each member must publish an article or give a speech about OOP once a month.

2. Each published article must reference at least one article of another member, otherwise it doesn't count.

3. Each member must react to each published article in its public comment section and everywhere, where it is cross-posted, including Twitter, Facebook, Hacker News, and Reddit. Also, shares and re-tweets are very desirable.

4. By the end of the month a Secretary writes up a summary of members' activity, suggests new members, and recommends who to eject. The Secretary is a volunteer role.

5. To join us a new member must submit a request to the Secretary, who will present the candidate for a democratic vote. A new member joins in with 51% of votes.

6. A member can be ejected by 64% of votes.
